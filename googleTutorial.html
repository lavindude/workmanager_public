To setup Google Calendar with this application, do the following:

1. First open Google Calendar.

2. Click on the 3 dots.

3. Then click 'Settings and sharing'

4. Once you are there, scroll down until you see something like this:

5. Copy that URL and paste it into the 'Google Calendar shared URL' input in the application.

6. Hit submit and the calendar should pop up.

If you would like to delete the existing calendar, click the 'Delete Existing Calendar URL'.
